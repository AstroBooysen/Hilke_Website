PhD Students

Hilke Brockmann supervises doctoral research on key social questions, with a focus on inequality, migration, worldviews, and social participation. Her doctoral students work on topics that connect contemporary social theory with empirically grounded research.

Samitha Udayanga

Migration of the highly educated

Samitha Udayanga’s doctoral research focuses on the migration of highly educated individuals. The project examines how highly skilled migrants navigate opportunity structures, mobility, and integration, and how education shapes migration trajectories and social outcomes.

View profile

Izudin Karic

Worldviews and meritocratic beliefs of young male Muslim men

Izudin Karic’s doctoral research explores worldviews and meritocratic beliefs among young male Muslim men. The project investigates how ideas of fairness, effort, belonging, and opportunity are shaped by social experience and how these beliefs connect to broader questions of identity, inequality, and integration.
