Research Interests

Hilke Brockmann’s research focuses on questions that matter directly to people’s lives. Her work brings together social science theory, innovative datasets, and advanced data science methods to better understand social change, inequality, migration, happiness, and political participation.

Aging, Happiness & Well-Being

A central focus of Hilke Brockmann’s research is the study of aging societies, quality of life, and subjective well-being. She examines when and why people are happy, how life chances and social structures shape well-being, and how aging populations experience social change across different contexts.

Inequality & Social Justice

Her research investigates the acceptance and rejection of economic inequalities, asking how social groups perceive fairness, privilege, and disadvantage. This work explores the cultural, structural, and behavioral foundations of inequality and how they influence public attitudes and everyday life.

Migration & Social Integration

Hilke Brockmann studies migration as a major social process that shapes both individual opportunities and collective futures. Her research examines what defines successful migration, how migrants navigate institutions and social structures, and how integration can be understood across different social groups.

Political Participation & Democracy

Another area of interest is how people can better participate politically. Her work considers how new forms of data, digital tools, and methodological innovations can deepen public participation, strengthen democratic processes, and reveal the intended and unintended consequences of political behavior.

Innovative Data & Methodological Approaches

Across all of these themes, Hilke Brockmann combines innovative datasets with cutting-edge data science models. She is interested in how new research methodologies can shed light on motives, behaviors, and large-scale social patterns, particularly among groups such as the elderly, migrants, women, and the socioeconomically advantaged and disadvantaged.
